A.P.J Abdul kalam

1931-2015

Missile Man of India

Dr.Avul pakir Jainulabden Abdul Kalam was the 11th President of India. His work with the Defence Research and Development Organisation in developing military equipment like missiles and a civilian space programme earned him the title of Missile Man of India. He also played a significant role in developing the first indigenous satellite launch vehicle.Thereafter, he came to be known as the Missile Man of India. He was a scientist in the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and the Defence Research and Development Organisation.
